What is spaghetti

Noodles and sauce and meat in ball form.

Here are the ingredients

  • Noodles
  • pasta sauce of your choosing
  • meatballs

How do I make the Spaghetti?

  1. Cook that noodles
  2. Cook them meatballs
  3. Heat the sauce.
  4. Combine
  5. Eat it.
